# URAI Genesis Master Spec

Status: BUILD-LOCKED  
Owner: Adam Clamp / URAI Labs

## Definition

URAI Genesis is a private, ad-free, passive life operating system where the user's real-world signals become a magical emotional world guided by an orb companion.

## Product lock

Home is the world. Orb is the guide. Galaxy is the life map. Mirror is reflection. Shadow is gentle truth. Legacy is memory. Passport is control. Council is inner guidance. Settings are trust.

## Required worlds

- Welcome / Onboarding
- Home World
- Orb Chat
- Galaxy
- Star Detail
- Mirror
- Shadow
- Legacy
- Passport
- Permissions
- Council
- Settings
- Why Am I Seeing This

## Non-negotiables

- URAI is ad-free.
- Passport ships at launch.
- Ground layer, orb, chat, and sound ship at launch.
- No demo, test, mock, placeholder, raw JSON, or debug labels appear in the user-facing app.
- Every insight has a Why Am I Seeing This explanation.
- Every permission is revocable.
- User can export and delete data.
- Shadow never accuses.
- Mirror never diagnoses.
- Council replaces OS Crew everywhere.
- Manual input is optional. Passive experience is primary.

## Forbidden user-facing language

Do not show these in production UI:

- demo
- test
- mock
- placeholder
- debug
- raw JSON
- tracking
- monitoring
- OS Crew

## Required privacy promise

URAI is ad-free. The user controls data, permissions, exports, and deletion choices. Nothing sensitive is shared without explicit permission.

## Required explanation standard

Every generated insight, star, forecast, shadow marker, notification, and permission suggestion must answer:

- What appeared?
- Why did it appear?
- What signals contributed?
- How confident is URAI in plain language?
- What else could explain it?
- How can the user hide, correct, revoke, export, or delete?

## Final Genesis completion test

Genesis is complete when the user can open the app, enter a beautiful living world, meet the orb, control permissions through Passport, tap the sky into Galaxy, open a star with an explanation, open Mirror, enter Shadow safely, view or generate Legacy scrolls, and feel that URAI is private, magical, alive, and controlled by them.
