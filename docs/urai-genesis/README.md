## Purpose

This folder contains the no-ambiguity Genesis/V1 build map for URAI.

URAI Genesis is a private, ad-free, passive life operating system where the user's real-world signals become a magical emotional world guided by an orb companion.

## Locked product sentence

Home is the world. Orb is the guide. Galaxy is the life map. Mirror is reflection. Shadow is gentle truth. Legacy is memory. Passport is control. Council is inner guidance. Settings are trust.

## Non-negotiables

- URAI is ad-free.
- Passport ships at launch.
- Ground layer, orb, chat, and sound ship at launch.
- No demo, test, mock, placeholder, raw JSON, or debug labels appear in the user-facing app.
- Every insight has a Why Am I Seeing This explanation.
- Every permission is revocable.
- User can export and delete data.
- Shadow never accuses.
- Mirror never diagnoses.
- Council replaces OS Crew everywhere.

## Build order

1. Foundation: routing, auth, Home, Orb, Permissions, Passport, Firestore schema, Sound manager, Settings.
2. Core Magic: Mood weather, Galaxy, Memory stars, Star detail, Why explanations, Mirror, Companion chat, empty states, permission-off states.
3. Depth: Shadow, Legacy, Council, Exports, Notifications, Recovery events, Consent history, delete flows.
4. Polish: animation, sound, copy, privacy, accessibility, responsiveness, performance, debug cleanup, QA.

## Final product test

A new user should enter URAI, meet the orb, choose permissions, see a living home world, tap the sky into Galaxy, open a star with an explanation, open Mirror, review Passport, and feel that the app is private, magical, alive, and controlled by them.
