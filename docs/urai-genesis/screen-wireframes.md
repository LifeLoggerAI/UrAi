# URAI Genesis Screen Wireframes

Each screen has a fixed purpose, entry, exit, state, and layout. Do not invent alternate names or generic dashboard versions.

## Welcome

Purpose: Introduce URAI as a private magical life OS.

Layout:

- Orb reveal centered.
- One sentence promise.
- CTA: Enter URAI.
- Small privacy promise below.

Primary copy:

> Welcome to URAI. A private world shaped by your life signals. Guided by your orb. Controlled by you.

## Onboarding

Seven panels:

1. Private world
2. Meet orb
3. Sky reflects emotional weather
4. Galaxy forms from meaningful signals
5. Passport keeps user in control
6. Choose starter permissions
7. Enter Home

## Home

Layout:

- Full-screen world.
- Sky top 55 percent.
- Ground bottom 30 percent.
- Orb centered at sky/ground threshold.
- Gateway glows: Mirror, Shadow, Legacy, Passport.
- Settings is subtle.

Interactions:

- Tap Orb: Orb Chat.
- Long press Orb: Council.
- Tap Sky: Galaxy.
- Tap Ground: Ground/recovery.
- Tap Mirror Glow: Mirror.
- Tap Shadow Ripple: Shadow.
- Tap Legacy Glow: Legacy.
- Tap Passport Sigil: Passport.

## Orb Chat

Layout: Bottom-sheet or full-screen glass chamber.

Modes:

- Ask URAI
- Daily Reflection
- Why Am I Seeing This
- Calm
- Replay
- Permissions

## Galaxy

Layout:

- Starfield canvas.
- Zoom levels.
- Clusters.
- Emotional color accents.
- Star tap opens Star Detail.

Empty state:

> Your sky is still forming. The first stars appear when URAI notices meaningful moments, rhythms, or reflections.

## Star Detail

Required fields:

- Title
- Date
- Emotional tone
- Why it appeared
- Source signals
- Confidence in plain language
- Related stars
- Actions

Actions:

- Ask URAI
- Save to Legacy
- Hide
- Correct
- Delete related insight
- Manage permissions

## Mirror

Sections:

- Today's Reflection
- Mood Weather
- Cognitive Rhythm
- Recovery Signal
- Relationship Tone
- Recent Pattern
- Why Am I Seeing This

## Shadow

Sections:

- Shadow Tension
- Behavioral Friction
- Attention Loops
- Digital Overload
- Avoidance / Silence Pattern
- Relief Path
- Why Am I Seeing This

Rule: Relief path is always visible. Never trap the user in darkness.

## Legacy

Sections:

- Life Chapters
- Recent Scrolls
- Seasonal Reflections
- Memory Exports
- Relationship Arcs
- Transformation Markers

## Passport

Sections:

- My Data
- Permissions
- Consent History
- Exports
- Identity
- Connected Sources
- Future Data Value
- Privacy Controls

## Permissions

Layout:

- Grouped toggles.
- Last used.
- Why URAI asks.
- Revoke.
- Delete related data.

Groups:

- Core App
- Passive Signals
- Intelligence Layers
- Sharing / Export
- Future / Advanced

## Council

Five guide chamber:

- Companion
- Mirror
- Guardian
- Archivist
- Ground

Do not use agent, bot, or OS Crew language.

## Settings

Sections:

- Account
- Appearance
- Sound
- Companion Tone
- Notifications
- Privacy
- Export / Delete
- Accessibility

## Why Am I Seeing This

Required structure:

- What appeared
- Why it appeared
- Contributing signals
- Confidence in plain language
- Alternative explanation
- User controls
