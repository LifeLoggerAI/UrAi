# URAI Product Boundaries Draft

This draft is for product review before professional legal review.

## URAI is

- A personal reflection and symbolic life-mapping app.
- A consent-based memory and insight interface.
- A privacy-controlled AI companion experience.

## URAI is not

- A medical device.
- A therapist.
- An emergency service.
- A diagnostic tool.
- A legal or financial advisor.
- A guarantee of truth about other people.
- A lie detector.
- A surveillance tool.

## Required boundary wording

URAI may help you reflect on patterns, but it should not be used as a substitute for professional care, emergency support, or medical advice.

## Sensitive interpretation boundaries

URAI should not claim certainty about another person's intent, truthfulness, diagnosis, health condition, or private thoughts. Reflections should use cautious language such as "may suggest," "might reflect," and "from what is visible."

## Emergency boundary

URAI is not emergency support. If a user may be in immediate danger, they should contact local emergency services or a trusted person nearby.
