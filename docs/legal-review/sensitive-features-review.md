# Sensitive Features Review

This is not legal advice. Sensitive features require professional review before enablement.

| Feature | Current Status | Risk | Required Review Before Enablement |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Shadow | Protected / demo-gated | Could surface sensitive emotional themes | Privacy, safety, consent, copy review |
| Legacy | Protected / demo-gated | Could involve family, memory, identity, or third-party content | Privacy, consent, export, inheritance/legacy wording review |
| Companion memory | Deferred/off by default | Persistent personalization and retention concerns | Consent, retention, deletion, AI safety review |
| Audio/transcripts | Deferred/off by default | Recording consent, bystander privacy, storage risk | Recording-law, platform, consent, retention review |
| Location | Deferred/off by default | Sensitive movement/location patterns | Consent, minimization, retention review |
| Health/body/somatic layer | Demo/sample only or limited | Could be misread as medical inference | Claims, consent, safety review |
| Relationship insights | Deferred or limited | Third-party privacy and harmful interpretation risk | Consent, defamation, safety, AI wording review |
| Exports | User-controlled/demo-safe | Private data may be shared outside URAI | Export warnings, deletion limits, third-party content review |
| Marketplace/data monetization future idea | Future/deferred | Privacy, consent, financial, platform concerns | Separate legal, privacy, and platform review |
