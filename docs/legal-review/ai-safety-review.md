# AI Safety Review

This is not legal advice. This document is for reviewing AI safety measures with counsel.

* **No Medical Advice**: The AI Companion is explicitly instructed not to provide medical or therapeutic advice.
* **Crisis Intervention**: The AI is designed to detect crisis language and refer users to appropriate professional help resources.
* **Content Filtering**: Input and output filtering is in place to block harmful or inappropriate content.
* **Human Review**: No human review of AI conversations is planned without explicit, opt-in user consent for specific troubleshooting or feedback purposes.
