# Data Retention Review

This is not legal advice. This document is for discussing data retention policy with counsel.

* **Waitlist Data**: Retained until user unsubscribes or project requests deletion.
* **User Account Data**: Retained until the user deletes their account.
* **Feedback Data**: May be retained indefinitely in an anonymized form for product improvement.
* **Backup Policy**: Counsel to advise on appropriate backup retention schedules.
