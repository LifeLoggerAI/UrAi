# Outreach Risk Claims Audit

This document is to be used to audit all outreach materials for claims that could be considered misleading or risky.

**Red Flag Keywords:**

* Therapy, diagnosis, treatment, cure, medical, clinical
* Always listening, detects depression, fixes your life
* Revolutionize, make a real difference, monetize your content

**Review Process:**

1. All outreach materials must be reviewed by the founder.
2. Any language that makes a health claim or promises an outcome will be removed.
3. The use of pressure tactics or manipulative language is prohibited.
4. We will be transparent and honest in all of our communications.
