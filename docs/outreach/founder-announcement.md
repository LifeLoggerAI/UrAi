## Long version

I’m Adam, the founder of URAI.

I’m building URAI because I believe AI should help people understand their lives without making them feel watched, judged, or reduced to a profile.

URAI Genesis is the first step: a private symbolic interface where your sky, Ground, Mirror, Companion, Passport, and Legacy are controlled by you.

Passport is our permission system. Nothing sensitive opens unless you choose to open it. The public demo uses sample data to show how this feels before asking anyone to open sensitive layers.

I’m looking for feedback on the core experience: does it feel clear, trustworthy, and meaningful?

Your feedback will help shape the final polish, sound design, privacy review, and accessibility testing for the full launch.

This is just the beginning, and I’m excited to build it with you.
