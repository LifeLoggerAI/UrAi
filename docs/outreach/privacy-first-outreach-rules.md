# Privacy-First Outreach Rules

* **Permission-First:** Always ask before adding someone to a mailing list or sending them unsolicited materials.
* **Transparency:** Be clear about who we are, what UrAI is, and what it is not. UrAI is for reflection, not diagnosis.
* **No Pressure:** Respect a person's right to decline our invitation. Avoid manipulative or emotional language.
* **User Control:** Emphasize that the user is in control of their data. UrAI is on-device and does not send personal data to the cloud.
* **Sample Data:** Our sample public demo uses sample data, not real user data.
* **No Health Claims:** Do not make any claims about health, wellness, or clinical outcomes. Do not use words like "therapy," "diagnosis," or "treatment."
* **Founder-Led:** Outreach should be personal and come from the founder.
