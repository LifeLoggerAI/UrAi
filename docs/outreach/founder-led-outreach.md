# Founder-Led Outreach

This is not legal advice.

- Founder is the primary point of contact for URAI Genesis.
- Outreach is relational, not transactional.
- Respectful, consent-driven, and transparent communication.
- No spam or unsolicited mass outreach.
- Clear opt-out and unsubscribe options.
- Waitlist and feedback are for listening, not just broadcasting.
- Honor do-not-contact requests.
