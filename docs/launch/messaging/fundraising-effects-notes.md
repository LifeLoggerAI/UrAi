# Fundraising Effects Notes

Use motion to support the story, not overwhelm it.

## Effects

- Scroll-triggered heartbeat glow.
- Tagline reveal as the page settles.
- Stretch goal unlock flare.
- Soft orb pulse near calls to action.
- Gentle bloom transitions between sections.

## Motion safety

- No aggressive flashing.
- No frantic shake effects.
- Reduced motion fallback required.
- Keep text readable at all times.
- Avoid motion that implies urgency or pressure.

## Fundraising tone

The page should feel founder-led, warm, and visionary. It should not use fear, manipulation, or guaranteed-outcome language.
