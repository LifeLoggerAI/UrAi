# URAI Product FAQ

## What is URAI?

URAI is a private AI companion and symbolic life map controlled by Passport permissions.

## How is URAI different from a journal?

A journal stores entries. URAI turns permissioned reflections into a living visual world with an orb, Life Map, Ground, Mirror, Passport, and Legacy archive.

## What is Passport?

Passport is the control center for what URAI can see, remember, reflect, use in AI replies, or export.

## Does URAI listen to me?

Not by default. Audio playback is different from audio recording. Any capture or transcript feature requires explicit permission.

## Does URAI use my email or location?

Only if you connect and open those layers. They stay closed by default.

## What is Shadow?

Shadow is a protected reflection layer for difficult or sensitive patterns. It stays closed unless you choose to open it.

## What is Legacy?

Legacy is an archive for moments you choose to carry forward. Nothing is carried forward automatically.

## Can I export my data?

Exports require review. URAI is designed so nothing leaves automatically.

## Can I use URAI without cloud sync?

Yes. URAI supports local-only use where available.

## Is URAI professional care?

No. URAI is for reflection. It does not diagnose, treat, or replace qualified support.

## Is the demo using real user data?

No. The public demo uses sample data.
