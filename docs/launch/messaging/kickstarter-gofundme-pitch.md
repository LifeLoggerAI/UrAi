# Kickstarter / GoFundMe Pitch

## Opening

Help build a private AI companion and symbolic life map that reflects your life without turning it into a dashboard.

## Problem

People are overwhelmed by apps that track, score, and notify them without helping them understand their lives gently. Many tools feel like pressure systems instead of places to return to.

## Solution

URAI creates a private Genesis world where users control what opens through Passport. Memory, mood, and meaning can become visible as a symbolic interface: an orb companion, Life Map, Ground, Mirror, Legacy archive, and reviewed exports.

## Core features

- Orb Companion
- Life Map
- Ground
- Mirror
- Passport
- Shadow
- Legacy
- Export Scrolls
- Rituals
- Notifications that stay quiet by default

## Why funding matters

Funding helps complete:

- final production assets
- audio and voice production
- Firebase/backend launch work
- privacy review
- accessibility testing
- public demo polish
- early user testing

## Reward ideas

- founder updates
- early access
- legacy supporter scroll
- behind-the-scenes build logs
- private demo sessions

## Boundaries

No equity or security promises. No medical claims. No guaranteed outcomes. URAI is for reflection, permission, and user-owned memory.
