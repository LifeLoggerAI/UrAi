# What URAI Is Not

URAI is a reflection product, not a professional care product.

URAI is not:

- a clinician or counselor
- a medical product
- a diagnosis product
- an urgent-response product
- a truth detector
- a monitoring app
- a substitute for qualified support
- a certainty engine about other people

## Preferred wording

URAI helps users reflect on patterns. It does not diagnose, treat, or determine truth.

## Copy rule

When describing sensitive features, use cautious language: “may help reflect,” “permissioned,” “sample data,” “private by default,” “user controlled,” and “symbolic.”
