# URAI Investor One-Pager

## Product

URAI is a private symbolic life interface: an AI companion, Life Map, Ground, Mirror, Passport, and Legacy system that helps users reflect through the layers they choose to open.

## Problem

People generate more personal signals than ever, but most products turn those signals into pressure, dashboards, or noise. Users need a calmer way to understand their lives without surrendering privacy or agency.

## Market direction

AI companions, personal data ownership, reflective wellness, memory tools, and consent-first personalization are converging. URAI sits at the intersection of private AI, symbolic UX, and user-owned memory.

## Differentiation

- Cinematic Genesis world instead of a dashboard.
- Passport-first permission architecture.
- Symbolic layers: Orb, Life Map, Ground, Mirror, Shadow, Legacy.
- Demo-first launch that uses sample data.
- Reflection language that avoids unsupported certainty claims.

## Privacy moat

URAI is designed around permissioned summaries, local-first behavior where possible, reviewed exports, and sensitive layers closed by default.

## User experience

URAI feels like a private visual world. Users open layers, talk to the orb companion, move through a Life Map, visit Ground, use Mirror, and decide what belongs in Legacy.

## Business model options

- freemium
- Pro subscription
- export packs
- private memory archive
- enterprise, coach, or research use later with consent-first boundaries
- data marketplace only future/back-burner, not V1 ads

## Current build status

URAI Genesis has a public demo route, launch page, waitlist, Passport/privacy foundation, Companion boundary behavior, admin controls, QA harness, and deploy documentation.

## Launch roadmap

1. Public demo and waitlist.
2. Early access cohort.
3. Privacy/accessibility review.
4. Pro/private archive experiments.
5. Expanded symbolic and export systems.
