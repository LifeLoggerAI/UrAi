# URAI Positioning

## Core positioning

URAI is a private symbolic life interface — an AI companion, Life Map, Ground, Mirror, Passport, and Legacy system that helps users reflect on their life through the layers they choose to open.

## Short tagline options

- Your life, reflected gently.
- A private sky for your inner world.
- The life map that only opens with permission.
- Where memory, mood, and meaning become visible.
- A private AI companion for reflection, not surveillance.

## One-line description

URAI turns permissioned personal signals into a private symbolic world — with an orb companion, Life Map, Ground, Mirror, Passport controls, and user-owned story artifacts.

## Longer description

URAI is a privacy-first AI companion and symbolic life map. Instead of showing users another dashboard, URAI gives them a living Genesis world: a sky that opens into a Life Map, a Ground layer for roots and recovery, a Mirror for gentle pattern reflection, a Passport for permissions, and a Legacy archive for moments they choose to carry forward.

## Messaging guardrails

- Reflection, not diagnosis.
- Permission, not surveillance.
- Symbolic insight, not certainty.
- User-owned memory, not hidden extraction.
- Calm interface, not pressure dashboard.
