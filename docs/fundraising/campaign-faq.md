# Campaign FAQ

## What is URAI Genesis?
A private symbolic life interface with an orb companion, Life Map, Passport, Ground, Mirror, Legacy system, and exportable story artifacts.

## Is URAI therapy?
No.

## Does URAI diagnose or treat anything?
No.

## Does URAI detect lies?
No.

## Is URAI always listening?
No. The campaign should not imply always-on listening.

## Does the demo use private data?
No. The public demo should use sample data only.

## Are supporters investing?
No. Contributions are support, not equity, securities, or investment contracts.

## Are outcomes guaranteed?
No.
