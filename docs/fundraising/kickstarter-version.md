# Kickstarter Version

## Project Title
Support URAI: A Permission-First AI Companion for Reflection, Memory, and Meaning

## Short Description
URAI Genesis is a private symbolic life interface with an orb companion, Life Map, Passport, and user-controlled story artifacts.

## Risks and Challenges
URAI is an early-stage product. Some systems may change as the demo is tested and simplified. Sensitive features remain permission-first and default off.

## Claims Boundary
URAI is not therapy, diagnosis, treatment, emergency support, lie detection, or a guaranteed outcome system. Support is not an investment, equity, or securities offering.
