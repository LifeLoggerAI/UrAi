# Founder Video Script

Hi, I’m Adam, founder of URAI.

I’m building URAI Genesis because I believe personal AI should feel private, meaningful, and user-controlled from the beginning.

URAI is a symbolic life interface: an orb companion, Life Map, Ground, Mirror, Passport, Legacy system, and exportable story artifact engine.

The most important promise is simple: nothing meaningful opens unless the user chooses to open it.

URAI is not therapy. It does not diagnose, treat, detect lies, provide emergency support, or promise guaranteed outcomes.

The public demo uses sample data so people can experience the world safely. This campaign helps polish the demo, strengthen privacy controls, improve the launch experience, and prepare URAI for early users.

If you believe AI should help people reflect without feeling watched, support URAI Genesis, try the demo, and join the waitlist.
