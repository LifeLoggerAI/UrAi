# Campaign Risk and Claims Audit

## Do Not Claim
- Therapy
- Diagnosis
- Treatment
- Lie detection
- Emergency support
- Always listening
- Guaranteed outcomes
- Investment returns
- Equity or securities
- Private user data exposure

## Safe Language
Use: reflection, memory, meaning, symbolic interface, private demo, sample data, permission-first, user-controlled, consent-led.

## Final Safety Statement
URAI is a private reflective interface. It is not therapy, diagnosis, treatment, emergency support, lie detection, or a guaranteed outcome system. The public demo uses sample data.
