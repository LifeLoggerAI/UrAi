## The Problem

Most apps turn human life into dashboards, scores, streaks, and alerts. They track more than they help. They notify more than they understand. And when AI enters the picture, people are right to ask: what can it see, what can it remember, and who controls it?

URAI’s answer: Passport first. Reflection without surveillance. Symbolic understanding without pressure.

## The Solution: URAI Genesis

URAI Genesis gives users a private symbolic space for reflection, memory, and meaning.
