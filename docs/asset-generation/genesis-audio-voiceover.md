# Genesis Audio and Voiceover

Voice direction: calm, intimate, cinematic, human. Not corporate. Not a hype announcer. The voice should sound like someone revealing the truth of the product, not selling software.

Actual final voiceover and music files were not generated in this environment. The audio assets are scripts, cue sheets, and captions for external production.
