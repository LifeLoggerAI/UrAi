# Genesis Music Direction

Create a cinematic emotional score that feels intimate first, huge second. Avoid corporate launch music and trailer cliches. The score should feel like memory becoming a world.

## Arc

- Opening: intimate pulse, sparse piano, soft synth, memory dust.
- URAI reveal: warm low brass or synth bloom, not triumphant hype.
- Life Map: celestial pads, soft arpeggios, relationship threads.
- Replay: emotional rise, heartbeat percussion, cinematic but restrained.
- Council/Ground: precise rhythmic pulse, intelligent machinery but human.
- Life Films/Music Videos: swelling cinematic beat, not cheesy.
- Passport: quiet trust, warm minimal tone.
- Final: huge but intimate resolve.

## Delivery Needed

- Full mix WAV, 48kHz, 24-bit.
- Stems: piano, pulse/percussion, pads, brass/synth bloom, texture, final resolve.
- 17 scene cutdowns matching the caption timecodes.
- No copyrighted sample packs without license proof.
