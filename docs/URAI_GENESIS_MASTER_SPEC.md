# URAI Genesis Master Spec

URAI Genesis is a passive, true-3D emotional/spatial AI life OS that reconstructs a user's life as a living memory field using passive signals, emotional weather, memory stars, relationship fields, recovery fields, narrator insights, and the Council/orb companion.

## Non-negotiable product rules

- The core home/world is true 3D: real camera movement, spatial depth, lighting, meshes, particles, portals, memory stars, fields, and orbiting companion behavior.
- Two-dimensional UI is reserved for overlays, menus, cards, settings, controls, and accessibility fallbacks.
- Production UI must not expose placeholder labels, debug text, dev wording, or fake system claims.
- URAI must feel magical, cinematic, spatial, minimal, polished, and emotionally safe.
- URAI remains ad-free.
- URAI Passport is launch-critical and controls consent, export, deletion, and data ownership posture.
- Visible product language uses "Council" instead of "OS Crew".
- Sensitive insights are permissioned, inferential, and worded safely.
- URAI must not state medical, diagnostic, deception, relationship, or crisis accusations as fact.

## Genesis systems

### World

The home scene is the living spatial root of URAI. It includes a ground layer, orb companion, emotional sky/galaxy, memory stars, mood weather field, portals, camera movement, ambient sound hooks, WebGL fallback, and minimal overlay UI.

### Signals

Passive signals are permissioned records from audio, transcription, movement, location, device/app context, calendar/email context, facial/environment inference, notification context, and user-seeded memories. Raw sensitive data should be minimized, referenced safely, and protected by grants.

### Intelligence

The intelligence layer converts permissioned signals into mood weather, mental-load snapshots, shadow cognition metrics, obscura patterns, cognitive stress signals, recovery events, narrator insights, memory stars, rituals, and relationship fields. All output language is inferential and non-diagnostic.

### Memory

Memory Galaxy / Life Map turns life data into symbolic stars, constellations, blooms, chapters, replay structures, timeline comparison, and Mirror of Becoming views.

### Companion and Council

The orb is the ambient companion. Council members are archetypal voices such as guardian, mirror, witness, strategist, healer, and scribe. They are warm, subtle, non-intrusive, and TTS-ready.

### Passport and permissions

URAI Passport gives grouped permission controls for audio/transcription, location/movement, device/app activity, calendar/email/context, social and relationship intelligence, mood and mental-load inference, facial/environment inference, notifications/narrator, exports/data ownership, and future external personalization/data marketplace permissions.

### Ritual and recovery

Rituals, ritual chains, recovery blooms, Threshold Mode, Restoration Story Paths, seasonal suggestions, Night Ritual Mode, symbolic rebirth, and Mirror exports connect to mood, mental load, memory stars, narrator insights, and companion state.

### Sound

Sound architecture includes ambient registry, orb cues, portal transitions, memory star cues, mood weather ambience, Council/chat cues, Mirror/rebirth cues, mute controls, and silent-safe fallback. No copyrighted audio is required or implied.

### Exports and admin

Exports include scrolls, memories, rituals, Passport data, and Mirror replays. Admin audit events must remain hidden from normal users and must not leak debug tools into production UI.

## Safety copy standard

Use: "signals", "patterns", "may indicate", "worth noticing", "possible shift", "gentle reflection".

Avoid: diagnosis, certainty, medical claims, surveillance language, crisis prediction as fact, and accusations such as lying, cheating, deception, abuse, or betrayal unless the user explicitly records their own lived statement and even then present it as user-provided context.

## Scaffold policy

A subsystem may be scaffolded in code or docs when live models, licensed audio, production permissions, or external integrations are not ready. Scaffolded status belongs in docs, comments, admin surfaces, or internal reports only, never in polished production UI.
